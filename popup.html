How to use Destiny Random Loadout

  • * You must be logged into Bungie's official gear manager and on the page itself
  • * Press the `Get Gear` button to fetch your currently available gear
  • * The page may need reloading if 'Get Gear' button is unresponsive
  • * Press the `Randomize` button to get a random loadout suggestion
  • * Use the `Move` or `Search` buttons to acquire weapons
  • * Make sure to wait between presses, `Move` takes some thought
